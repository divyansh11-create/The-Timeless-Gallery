Canvas of Time

A Virtual Art Gallery celebrating classic & contemporary visions.

About This Gallery

Canvas of Time is a simple virtual gallery designed to showcase a curated selection of paintings, digital artworks, photography, and DIY creations. This gallery is built using only HTML to keep the experience lightweight and accessible to learners and art lovers alike.

Curatorial Statement

Our aim is to present works that explore memory, space, and imagination. From Van Gogh's swirling skies to Dalí's dreamlike clocks, each piece invites reflection on time and perception. We place equal value on historical masterpieces and modern digital expression.

Featured Artists

  • Vincent van Gogh — Starry Night
  • Salvador Dalí — The Persistence of Memory
  • Contemporary digital artists — Digital Abstract
  • Community contributors — DIY Craft selections

How to Explore

  1. Return to the Gallery Home to view artworks and read descriptions.
  2. Click image links to learn more about each work (external links open in a new tab).
  3. Use the comparison table on the main page to compare mediums and years.

Contribute

Would you like your work featured in the gallery? We welcome submissions from hobbyists and emerging creators. To submit:

  • Prepare a high-resolution image (JPG/PNG) and a short description (title, medium, year).
  • Send the image and details to: submissions@example.com
  • Include a short artist statement
